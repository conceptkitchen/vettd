4. Include the primary keyword in the title, first paragraph, and at least 2 subheadings

7. Include internal linking suggestions (placeholder format: [LINK: anchor text])
8. Add a FAQ section at the end with 3-5 relevant questions
9. Maintain a conversational but authoritative tone
10. End with a clear call-to-action

Formatting requirements:
- Use short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Include bullet points and numbered lists where appropriate
- Bold important phrases for scanability
- Target a Flesch reading score of 60-70
